# 131. Palindrome Partitioning

## 题目链接

- https://leetcode.com/problems/palindrome-partitioning/

## 题目要求

## 示例

### 示例 1

- 输入：`s = "aab"`
- 输出：`[['a', 'a', 'b'], ['aa', 'b']]`
